Quick Answers

Frequently Asked

Where do you ship to?

All retail orders ship globally through our Amazon storefront — currently Amazon US, UK, EU, Canada, and Australia. For wholesale, we ship from our Colombo warehouse to most countries directly.

How is Ceylon cinnamon different from regular supermarket cinnamon?

Most "cinnamon" sold in supermarkets is cassia — a different (and cheaper) species. Ceylon cinnamon (Cinnamomum verum) is the only "true" cinnamon: sweeter, more delicate, and significantly lower in coumarin, making it daily-safe for regular consumption.

Are your products organic?

All our farms practice organic-by-tradition farming — polyculture, no synthetic pesticides or fertilisers. Several of our partner farms hold formal organic certification; the others operate to identical standards but choose not to bear the certification cost.

What's your return policy?

All Amazon orders are protected by Amazon's standard 30-day return guarantee, plus our own no-questions-asked replacement promise. If a harvest doesn't delight you, we'll replace or refund — full stop.

Do you offer wholesale or B2B pricing?

Yes — we work with restaurants, cafés, tea rooms, and specialty grocers. Use the contact form above and select "Wholesale / B2B" — we'll send our wholesale catalogue and minimum order details within one business day.

How long do your spices and tea stay fresh?

Whole spices keep for 24 months stored airtight, away from light. Ground spices peak within 12 months. Loose-leaf tea is best within 18 months. Each tin is dated with its harvest week — so you know exactly how fresh it is.
